Farah Visual Arts

Welcome to Farah Visual Arts founded by mixed media artist and designer Farah Soobhan.

Concept and Ideas

Farah uses a blend of collage, typography and painting to create pop art pieces based on important topics like religion, political and humanitarian crises. She hopes her work will facilitate reflection and enable conversations to bridge gaps between communities.

Workshops, Tutoring and Prints

Please get in touch using the contact form for enquiries about workshops, bespoke one-to-one art tutoring or to request price lists for merchandise, originals, as well as prints. Farah is also always open to collaborating on new projects, one off commissions and related ventures.
